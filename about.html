This is a website where you can download any books you want, as a PDF file. You no longer need a bunch of Apps on your cellphone for different types of books. Because the most irrating thing is when you download a Book-App but it does not have the books you actually want. So I decided to create a website for my fellow readers who has a similar issue with Apps.

  • The website is quick and easy to use.
  • Anyone can just login to it
  • PDF files arent big, so if u downlaod a book, it wont take that much space
  • Its not a App so it wont be on your cellphone and take up space
